Delete your Patterns account and data

Patterns, by Booftech Limited. How to permanently delete your account and the data linked to it.

Delete from inside the app

  1. Open Patterns and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and personal data are deleted right away.

If you cannot open the app

Email privacy@patternsproject.co.za from the address on your account and ask us to delete it. We action these requests within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Your account and email address
  • The photos you uploaded
  • Your sightings and the names you gave to giraffes
  • The location data attached to those sightings
  • Your device push-notification token

What may be kept

Anonymous, aggregated conservation records (for example, that a giraffe was seen in a region on a date, with no link to you) may be retained to support giraffe conservation. Any personal copies in routine backups are purged within 90 days.
